Agustin Bacugan III

Bucky is an experienced litigator with an extensive background in the prosecution and defense of civil, criminal, labor and intellectual property cases. He sub-specializes in inter-corporate and labor-management negotiations as a mode of concluding mutually beneficial contracts or collective bargaining agreements. Moreover, he is proficient in conducting public interest litigation. Bucky began his legal career as an apprentice for Angara Abello Concepcion Regala & Cruz (ACCRA). After obtaining his Juris Doctor degree from the Ateneo de Manila School of Law, he became an associate in Escudero Marasigan Vallente & E.H. Villareal (EMSAVVIL). He was the Senior Partner in Fong Bacungan & Associates until joining the firm. Bucky obtained his degree in Bachelor of Arts ( Major in Social Science) from the Ateneo de Manila University in 1998. He was admitted to the Philippine Bar in 2001. Bucky handles litigation and corporate accounts.

Contact Bucky by email at acb@bsalaw.ph or call (632) 8571 48 86.

Angelyn H. Sy

Anj took the Philippine Bar Examinations in 2018. She obtained her Bachelor of Laws degree from the San Beda College of Law, Alabang. She was an active student leader. Anj served as Vice President of the law school student government in 2015 and became the President in 2016. Anj holds a Doctor of Pharmacy Degree from the Centro Escolar University, Makati as well as a Bachelor of Science degree in Pharmacy from the University of Santo Tomas. She speaks English, Filipino and basic Mandarin. Anj handles corporate clients and some litigation
